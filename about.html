About

I am motivated to work and learn in a collaborative, dynamic, and progressive environment. Strong organizational, oral/visual presentation, and problem-solving skills support my efforts to deliver high-quality products and service excellence.

I use Adobe Creative Suite software programs to create a range of products in videography, motion graphics, web design, and interactive content development. My experience includes developing promotional strategies to elevate brand awareness, encourage customer engagement, and increase online traffic. Some of my works provide a message; my best tell a story.
